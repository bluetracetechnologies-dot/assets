# Project Summary

## Project Name
Wellness Mobile App MVP to Launch

## Objective
Deliver a launch-ready wellness mobile MVP for iOS and Android with mood tracking, meditation content, personalized recommendations, secure backend, and store release support.

## Scope Focus
- Product-first mobile flow design and execution
- Mood check-ins, mood history, and personalized recommendation loop
- Meditation content library and content administration pipeline
- Notifications, analytics, and privacy-aware data handling
- iOS and Android build, QA, and store launch support

## Execution Phases
1. Discovery, UX flow, and wellness architecture
2. UI/UX design and clickable prototype
3. Mobile MVP build with onboarding, mood tracking, and meditation library
4. Backend, recommendations, and admin/content panel
5. Testing, analytics, notifications, and privacy hardening
6. App Store and Play Store launch support

## Deliverables
- Cross-platform mobile app MVP (iOS/Android)
- Backend services, data model, and admin/content panel
- Test report, analytics setup, and privacy checklist
- App Store and Play Store submission support artifacts

## Commercial Baseline
