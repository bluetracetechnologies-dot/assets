# Technical Requirements Checklist

## Architecture and Existing Assets
- Confirm target schematic revision, PCB stack-up, and mechanical outline
- Confirm nRF52840 module or discrete RF path details
- Confirm LoRa transceiver, antenna choice, and placement constraints
- Confirm available BOM, footprint library, and layout source files

## Interfaces and Features
- Clarify sensor interfaces, GPIO mapping, and programming/debug header needs
- Confirm power-tree goals, battery type, regulator choice, and sleep-current target
- Confirm RF keepout, antenna clearance, and ground-return expectations
- Confirm fabrication constraints for JLCPCB panelization and assembly

## Validation and Acceptance
- Define review acceptance criteria for schematic, layout, and BOM
- Confirm expected evidence format: screenshots, redline notes, and annotated checklists
- Confirm whether impedance, RF tuning, or thermal checks are required
- Confirm final signoff and revision-control process

## Delivery and Release
- Confirm file delivery format and preferred export types
- Confirm annotation style for corrections and review comments
- Confirm handover artifacts and support window
- Confirm final signoff process
